This is the official space of ChEBI on Github. 

ChEBI (**Ch**emical **E**ntities of **B**iological **I**nterest) is an open-access database and ontology of chemical entities. The term ["chemical entity"](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/chebi/CHEBI:24431) in ChEBI refers to any constitutionally or isotopically distinct atom, molecule, ion, ion pair, radical, radical ion, complex, conformer, etc. The term also includes groups (part- molecular entities), chemical substances, and classes of molecular entities.

Our main repositories are maintained in a self-hosted EBI Gitlab instance, they are:

* [ChEBI Ontology Generation](https://gitlab.ebi.ac.uk/chembl/chebi/chebi-2.0/chebi-ontology-generator)
* [Backend](https://gitlab.ebi.ac.uk/chembl/chebi/chebi-2.0/chebi-backend)
* [Frontend](https://gitlab.ebi.ac.uk/chembl/chebi/chebi-2.0/chebi-frontend)
* [Database Management](https://gitlab.ebi.ac.uk/chembl/chebi/chebi-2.0/chebi-data-management)
* [Dumps Generation](https://gitlab.ebi.ac.uk/chembl/chebi/chebi-2.0/chebi-dumps)
* [Tracker](https://github.com/ebi-chebi/ChEBI) (Open new issues here)

Take a look our data products [here](https://ftp.ebi.ac.uk/pub/databases/chebi/).
